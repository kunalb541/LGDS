Dear Editor,

Please consider the enclosed manuscript, "Task-Dependent Optimal Linear
Observers in Linear Gaussian Dynamical Systems," for publication in
Systems & Control Letters.

The paper studies observer selection in stationary finite-dimensional
linear Gaussian dynamical systems. Its main result is an exact
characterization of target-dependent optimal rank-r linear observers:
for prediction target C x(t+tau), the optimal observer is the top-r
eigenspace of the associated target information matrix, and a common
optimal observer across a target family exists if and only if the
family is r-coherent.

The manuscript is concise, theorem-driven, and scoped to fixed-rank
linear observers and squared-error prediction in the LGDS setting.
We believe this focus is well aligned with the aims of Systems &
Control Letters.

This manuscript is not under consideration elsewhere. No external
funding was received for this work, and the author declares no
competing interests.

Thank you for your consideration.
